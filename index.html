About the event

What is Vibe-A-Thon?

Vibe-A-Thon '26 is a fast-paced, six-hour sprint where student builders turn an idea into a working AI prototype before the day is out. No multi-day grind, no bloated scope — just focused, high-energy building alongside mentors, peers and a panel that rewards genuinely useful solutions.

Whether you're fine-tuning a model, wiring up an API, or shipping your first computer-vision demo, the format is built to compress months of learning into a single unforgettable afternoon.
